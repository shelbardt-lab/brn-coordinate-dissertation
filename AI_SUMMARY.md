# AI Summary

## Main Research Question
How does BRN-Coordinate recruit, mobilize and sustain insurgency in Southern Thailand?

## Key Findings
- BRN developed a decentralized insurgent structure.
- Religious schools played an important role in socialization.
- Recruitment relied heavily on local social networks.
- Political violence was strategically employed.
- State responses often strengthened insurgent narratives.
